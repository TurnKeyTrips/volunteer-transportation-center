Our events do more than bring the North Country together. Every dollar raised goes to mileage reimbursement for our volunteer drivers, and every mile they drive is a neighbor getting to a medical appointment, the pharmacy, or the grocery store.

Some of our events are decades-old traditions, from chili cook-offs and golf tournaments to trail runs and trap shoots, and there is always something new in the works. Come hungry, bring a friend, and help keep your neighbors moving.
