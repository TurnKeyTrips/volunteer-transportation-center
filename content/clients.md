The Volunteer Transportation Center provides rides to NNY neighbors who otherwise would not have access to critical medical, mental health, dental, and other life-sustaining services. Our services are provided to those in Jefferson, Lewis, and St. Lawrence Counties **regardless of ability to pay**. We also have contracts with municipal insurance companies (for example, New York State Medicaid) and many human service agencies.

If you need a ride to an upcoming medical appointment, call the appropriate number below between 8am and 5pm, Monday through Friday. [**Not a client yet? Fill out a new client application**](https://form.jotform.us/61025552290145)
