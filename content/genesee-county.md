## Interested in becoming a Genesee County Volunteer Driver?

**Email us: [info@volunteertransportation.org](mailto:info@volunteertransportation.org)**

Our volunteers use their own vehicles to transport neighbors in Genesee County to critical medical, dental, mental health and other life sustaining services. Volunteers are reimbursed up to standard IRS mileage rate. The ideal Volunteer must:

- like to drive
- be willing to give a minimum of 4 hours per week
- have a clean driving record
- pass a thorough background check
- and have a love of giving back to the community

### Three Ways to Apply

1. [Online New Volunteer Driver Application](https://form.jotform.com/61038212879155)
2. [Downloadable New Volunteer Driver Form](/files/driver-application-form.pdf)
3. Call our Genesee County Program at (585) 250-5030
