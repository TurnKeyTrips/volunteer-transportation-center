## Interested in becoming a St. Lawrence County Volunteer Driver?

Our volunteer drivers use their own vehicles to transport neighbors in St. Lawrence County to critical medical, dental, mental health and other life sustaining services. Drivers are reimbursed at the standard IRS mileage rate. The ideal Volunteer Driver must:

- be willing to give a minimum of 4 hours per week
- have a clean driving record
- pass a thorough background check
- and have a love of giving back to the community

### Three Ways to Apply

1. [Online New Driver Application](https://form.jotform.com/61038212879155)
2. [Downloadable New Driver Form](/files/driver-application-form.pdf)
3. Call our [St. Lawrence County Office](mailto: info@volunteertransportation.org) at (315) 714-2034
