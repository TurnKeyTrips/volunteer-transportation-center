The Volunteer Transportation Center provides rides to people in our community facing the barrier of transportation. Every dollar raised by the Volunteer Transportation Center Foundation goes to mileage reimbursement for our dedicated volunteer drivers, making this transportation possible.

Many of our events are annual traditions, from chili and golf to trail runs and trap shoots, and new ones are always in the works.
