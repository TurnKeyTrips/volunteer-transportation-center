The 27th Annual North Country Chili Cook-Off brought amateur and professional chili teams together for a great cause. Watertown International Airport took home the People's Choice Award, Samaritan Medical Center won the Professional category, and Lowville American Legion Post 162 topped the Amateur category.

Every dollar raised goes to mileage reimbursement for our dedicated volunteer drivers. Thank you to every team, taster, and sponsor who came out to support rides for our neighbors.
